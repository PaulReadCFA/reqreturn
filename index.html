Required return and dividend projection over time

This chart displays the projected dividend cash flows over 10 years with the calculated required return. At year 0, the initial stock purchase price is shown as a negative cash flow (money paid out). Then, at each subsequent year, dividend payments grow at the specified growth rate. The dashed blue line shows the constant required return percentage on the right axis.

Keyboard navigation: Tab to focus the chart. Use Left and Right arrow keys to move between years. Press Home to jump to year 0, or End to jump to the final year. Each year will announce its dividend amount, required return, and total cash flow.

Note: Values in parentheses indicate negative cash flows (outflows). Hover over values to see calculations. Press Escape to exit table navigation.
